# Build Your Website Alone or Hire a Digital Agency?

You're ready to launch your online presence. But here comes the big question: should you build your own website using a drag-and-drop builder, or invest in a professional digital agency? Both paths can lead to success — but they don't suit the same business profiles, budgets, or goals.

In this guide, we break down the real trade-offs so you can make an informed decision.

## The DIY Route: Wix, Squarespace, or WordPress

Website builders like Wix, Squarespace, and WordPress have democratised web publishing. For roughly €10–30/month, you get hosting, templates, and a visual editor.

### When DIY makes sense

- **You're a freelancer or solo creator** with a simple portfolio or landing page
- **You have time to learn** — expect 20–40 hours for a decent 5-page site
- **Your budget is under €500** and you don't need custom functionality
- **You want full autonomy** over updates without depending on a third party

### The real hidden costs of DIY

It looks free, but it isn't. Premium templates cost €50–200. Plugins for SEO, forms, or e-commerce add €10–50/month each. And most importantly: your time has value. If you spend 40 hours building a mediocre website instead of selling your services, you've paid a steep hidden cost.

## Hiring a Digital Agency: What You Actually Get

A professional agency doesn't just "put together a website." They bring strategy, design expertise, technical performance, and SEO from day one.

### What an agency delivers

- **Custom UX/UI design** tailored to your brand and target audience
- **SEO-optimised architecture** from the first page
- **Performance optimisation** — fast loading, mobile-first, Core Web Vitals compliance
- **Integrations** (CRM, payment, booking, live chat) handled correctly
- **Ongoing support** so you're never stuck alone with a broken plugin

A Mindzy website project typically starts at €1,500 for a local business and scales with complexity. You get a result that works — technically, visually, and commercially.

### When to choose an agency

- You're launching a business and first impressions matter
- You sell products or services online and conversions are critical
- You've already tried DIY and you're not happy with the result
- You don't have the time or inclination to manage technical details

## The True Cost Comparison

| | DIY | Agency |
|---|---|---|
| Upfront cost | €0–500 | €1,500–8,000+ |
| Monthly cost | €30–100 | €50–200 (maintenance) |
| Your time | 40–100h | Near zero |
| Result quality | Variable | Professional |
| SEO out of the box | Weak | Strong |
| Scalability | Limited | High |

## GEO Optimisation: Why It Matters in 2026

Whether you build it yourself or through an agency, your site's visibility in **AI-powered search** is now a critical factor. Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, and Perplexity increasingly answer user questions by pulling from well-structured, authoritative web pages.

An agency-built site typically includes the structured data (Schema.org), clear FAQ sections, and authoritative content that these AI engines favour. A DIY site rarely gets this right without significant expertise.

**Practical tip:** Regardless of which route you choose, make sure your site includes LocalBusiness structured data, an FAQ section, and clear, factual service descriptions. These are the building blocks of GEO visibility.

## Our Verdict

If you're just starting out with a micro-budget and a simple need, a DIY tool is a valid first step. But if you want a site that genuinely grows your business — converting visitors into clients, ranking on Google, and making the right first impression — partnering with a digital agency is the smarter investment.
