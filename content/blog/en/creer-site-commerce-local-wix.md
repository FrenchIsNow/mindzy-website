# How to Create a Local Commerce Website with Wix

Wix is one of the most beginner-friendly website builders available, and it's particularly well-suited for local businesses that need a clean, professional online presence quickly and without technical complexity.

## Is Wix Right for Your Local Business?

Wix works well for:
- Restaurants, cafés, and food businesses
- Retail shops wanting an online catalogue or booking system
- Local service providers (plumbers, electricians, cleaners)
- Hairdressers, beauty salons, and wellness businesses
- Freelancers and consultants in local markets

Wix may not be the right choice if: you need serious SEO power, plan to run a large e-commerce operation, or want to own and control your platform long-term.

## Step-by-Step: Build Your Wix Site

### Step 1: Create Your Wix Account

Go to wix.com and create a free account. You can build and test your site for free; you'll need a paid plan to connect your own domain and remove Wix branding.

### Step 2: Choose Between Wix ADI and Wix Editor

- **Wix ADI (AI-powered):** Answer questions about your business and Wix generates a customised site automatically. Good starting point, easy to customise afterwards.
- **Wix Editor:** Full drag-and-drop editor starting from a template. More control, slightly steeper learning curve.

For most local businesses, Wix ADI is the fastest path.

### Step 3: Select the Right Template

Wix has hundreds of templates organised by industry. Find your industry category (Restaurant, Health & Wellness, Professional Services, etc.) and choose a template close to your desired result.

**Important:** You cannot switch templates after publishing — choose carefully.

### Step 4: Essential Pages for Local Commerce

- **Home:** Business name, what you do, location, phone number visible in header
- **Services/Products:** What you offer with descriptions and prices
- **About:** Your story, your team, your values
- **Contact & Location:** Address, phone, opening hours, Google Map embed
- **Booking/Reservations:** If applicable — Wix has a built-in booking system (Wix Bookings)

### Step 5: Add Key Wix Apps

From the Wix App Market:
- **Wix Bookings** — appointment scheduling (built-in, free to add)
- **Wix Stores** — if you want to sell products online
- **Wix Blog** — for content marketing and SEO
- **Wix Live Chat** — real-time chat with visitors
- **Google Maps** — embed your location map

### Step 6: Set Up Local SEO

In Wix SEO Settings:
- Page title: "[Business Name] — [City] — [Main Service]"
- Meta description: include your city and key service
- Add your business address to the Wix Local SEO tool

### Step 7: Connect Your Domain and Publish

Upgrade to a paid plan (from €10/month for Business Basic), connect your existing domain or purchase one through Wix, and publish.
