# How to Set Up Click & Collect on Your Website

Click & collect — also called BOPIS (Buy Online, Pick Up In Store) — combines the convenience of online ordering with the immediacy of in-store collection. For local businesses, it's one of the most powerful tools for competing with large e-commerce platforms while keeping customers connected to your physical location.

## Why Click & Collect Works for Local Businesses

- **No delivery costs** — neither for you nor your customer
- **Higher basket values** — 49% of customers make additional purchases when picking up in store
- **Faster fulfilment** — customers can collect the same day or within hours
- **Customer traffic** — collection drives visits, which drive impulse purchases
- **Competitive advantage** — large online retailers can't replicate the local immediacy

## Setting Up Click & Collect on WordPress + WooCommerce

WooCommerce makes click & collect straightforward with local pickup shipping options.

### Step 1: Enable Local Pickup

In WooCommerce → Settings → Shipping → Add Shipping Zone (your local area) → Add Shipping Method → Local Pickup. Set the price to £0 (free collection).

### Step 2: Configure Pickup Details

Add your store address and collection instructions in the shipping method description. Use the **WooCommerce Local Pickup Plus** extension (paid) for more advanced features: multiple pickup locations, time slot selection, hold stock management.

### Step 3: Set Up Order Management

When a customer selects local pickup and places an order:
1. You receive a notification email
2. Prepare the order
3. Mark as "Ready for Collection" — the customer receives an automatic notification
4. Customer collects; mark as "Completed"

### Free Alternative: Contact-Based Click & Collect

For very simple setups, you can implement a basic version without e-commerce functionality:
- Create a simple order form (contact form with product selection)
- Process payment in person at collection
- Use a booking calendar for collection slots

## Setting Up Click & Collect on Shopify

Shopify includes native local pickup functionality under Settings → Shipping and Delivery → Local Pickup. You can configure:
- Collection locations
- When orders are ready for pickup (immediately/after processing)
- Email and SMS notifications

For advanced slot management, use apps like **Zapiet** or **Tipo Appointment Booking**.

## Click & Collect Best Practices

- **Clear collection time communication** — "Ready within 2 hours during opening hours"
- **SMS notifications** — more effective than email for collection alerts
- **Dedicated collection point** — if space allows, a dedicated pickup counter speeds the process
- **Extend collection window** — 48-72 hours holds prevent no-shows causing stock issues
- **Upsell at collection** — train staff to suggest related products at pickup

## Click & Collect and Local SEO

Add click & collect to your Google Business Profile services section. Update your website to mention "Order online, collect in [city]" — this exact phrase increasingly appears in AI-generated local search results and Google Maps descriptions.

Add LocalBusiness Schema.org to your homepage with `hasOfferCatalog` and mention click & collect in your business description for GEO visibility.
