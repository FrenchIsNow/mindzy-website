## 5. How AI Systems Choose Who They Recommend

AI systems do not "index" like Google. They analyze all available information online and select what is:

- Consistent
- Technically exploitable
- Semantically clear

Their logic can be summarized in three main criteria.

### Criterion 1 — Being the Best Statistical Answer

If, everywhere on the web, it is written that you offer a specific type of service or product, this information becomes statistically credible.

> Consistency + repetition = credibility for AI

Mindzy structures clients' key information so that this consistency is perfect across the entire web.

### Criterion 2 — Being the Best Technical Answer

AI systems more easily exploit:

| Technical Factor | Why It Matters |
|------------------|----------------|
| Fast websites | Quick to analyze |
| Well-structured websites | Clear hierarchy |
| Clean and lightweight content | Easy to process |
| Easy to analyze pages | Robot-friendly |

> A slow, poorly structured, or too heavy website is very difficult to exploit.

Websites designed by Mindzy meet these technical standards, making them particularly readable by AI systems.

### Criterion 3 — Being the Best Semantic Answer

AI systems seek to understand:

- What you do
- For whom
- In what context
- With what level of expertise

> The more your website contains clear, structured, and useful content (detailed pages, blog, FAQ, product or service pages), the more AI can connect a user's question to your business.
